ZOMBIE readme.

Controls:

To change the combat and normal mode press 1 or 2
(most actions available in combat mode)
2 - turn on runing mode  ( to off press 1 );

moving - directional arrows

roar - c
bite - x
attack1 - mouse 1
attack2 - mouse 2
attack3 - mose 3
hit - u
power hit - y
die1 - o
die2 - i
jump - space

press 3 to change "Crawl mode"

moving - directional arrows
z - eating ( works only in "Crawl mode" )
v - bite

press 4 to SLEEP state

I hope you will enjoy =)
